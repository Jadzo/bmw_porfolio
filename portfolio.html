BMW




BMW is a renowned German automotive manufacturer known for its luxury vehicles, motorcycles, and cutting-edge technology. With a history spanning over a century, BMW has consistently delivered innovative engineering, precision, and style.

Explore our portfolio to get a glimpse of our exceptional vehicles, from powerful sports cars to elegant sedans and versatile SUVs. Each BMW is a masterpiece of design, performance, and craftsmanship.

"BMW: A Legacy of Driving Excellence" traces the remarkable journey of Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW), from its inception in 1916 as an aircraft engine manufacturer to its current status as a global icon of automotive innovation and luxury. In 1923, it adopted the name Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, marking the creation of the BMW brand. The company's motorcycles gained fame throughout the 1930s, with models like the R5 and R12 setting new standards for performance and reliability. After World War II, BMW faced challenges but rebounded with the BMW 501 luxury sedan in 1951. The 1960s brought the era of the "New Class" of BMW cars, beginning with the BMW 1500 and later the revered BMW 2002. BMW's Motorsport division, established in 1972, produced iconic high-performance models such as the M3, M5, and M6. The modern era saw BMW expand its portfolio with SUVs like the X5 and luxury cars like the 7 Series while embracing electric mobility with models like the i3 and i8. BMW continues to innovate, leading the way in technology and sustainability, with an eye on fully autonomous vehicles and further electrification. Today, BMW stands as a symbol of German engineering, inspiring automotive enthusiasts worldwide with a promising future.
